## Introduction

Conformal field theory is a quantum field theory invariant under conformal (angle-preserving) transformations, which powerfully constrains correlation functions and often makes theories exactly solvable, especially in two dimensions. It underpins string worldsheet dynamics and captures universality classes at critical points in statistical and condensed matter systems.
