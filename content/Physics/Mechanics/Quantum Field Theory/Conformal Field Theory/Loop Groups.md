> [!definition] Loop Group
> Suppose $G$ is a finite dimensional, compact, simple, [[The Fundamental Group#^3b18ee|simply connected]] Lie group, called the *gauge group*. Then the *loop group*  of $G$ is the [[Groups, Order and Subgroups#^6e0960|group]] of smooth maps from the circle $S^{1}$ to $G$.
>
