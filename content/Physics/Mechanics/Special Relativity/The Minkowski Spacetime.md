> [!definition] Minkowski Spacetime
> The Minkowski spacetime is a flat $4$-dimensional space $\R^4$ endowed the [[Riemannian Metrics#^c3ac13|metric]] $$\d s^{2}=-(\d x^{0})^{2}+(\d x^{1})^{2}+(\d x^{2})^{2}+(\d x^{3})^{2}.$$Physically, $x^{0}$ is the time coordinate and $x^{1},x^{2},x^{3}$ are the spatial coordinates, and we usually write $(ct,x,y,z)$.
