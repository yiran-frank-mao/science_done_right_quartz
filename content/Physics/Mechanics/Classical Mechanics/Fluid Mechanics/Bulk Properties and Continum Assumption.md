**Def**  <i><u>Bulk(Macroscopic) Properties</u></i>
Bulk properties of some fluid are pressure, temperature, density and velocity.

**Assum**  <i><u>Continum Assumption</u></i>
Bulk/macroscopic properties are assumed to be well-defined at infinitesimal volume elements. It holds for scales that are large compared to intermolecular distances.
