**Thrm** <i><u> Bernoulli's Principle</u></i>
The following equation holds along streamlines in steady flow of constant density:
$$ P + \rho \phi + \frac{1}{2}\rho |\vec u|^2= \mathrm{Const} $$
where $\phi$ is the gravitational potential.

**Thrm**  <i><u>Kutta-Jowkowski Lift Theorem</u></i>
The lift force per length of a plane is given by:
$$ \mathsf{f}_{\text{lift}} = \pi \rho u^2 \alpha L \quad\text{(N/m)}$$
