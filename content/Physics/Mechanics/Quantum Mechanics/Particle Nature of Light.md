## Photoelectric Effect
![|400](https://i.imgur.com/qd6A7gK.png)

>[!principle] Planck–Einstein Relation
>According to special relativity, photon energy is directly proportional to frequency:$$E = h\nu=\hbar \omega= \frac{hc}{\lambda}.$$

>[!corollary] 
> The momentum of a photon is then given by $p=\hbar k=\frac{h}{\lambda}$.

## The Compton Effect

>[!theorem] 
>**Thrm**  <i><u>Compton Scattering Formula</u></i>
>The Compton scattering formula is:$$\omega-\omega^{\prime}=\frac{2\hbar}{mc^2}\omega\omega^{\prime}\sin^{2}\frac\theta2$$ which is equivalent in terms of wavelength: $$\lambda^{\prime}-\lambda=2\frac{2\pi\hbar}{mc}\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2}$$where $\lambda_{c}=\frac{2\pi\hbar}{mc}$ is the Compton wavelength of a particle with mass $m$.
