
**Law**  <i><u>Lambert's Law</u></i>
Lambert's (cosine) law says that the radiant intensity observed from an ideal diffusely reflecting surface or ideal diffuse radiator is directly proportional to the cosine of the angle $θ$ between the observer's line of sight and the surface normal.

**Def**  <i><u>Lambertian Surface</u></i>
A surface which obeys Lambert's law is said to be Lambertian, and exhibits Lambertian reflectance.

**Prop**  A Lambertian surface has the same radiance when viewed from any angle.
