> [!definition] Planar Graph
> A *planar graph* is a graph that can be embedded in the plane, meaning it can be drawn on a flat surface without any edges crossing each other. Formally, a graph $G=(V,E)$ is *planar* if there exists an injective function $f\colon V \to \mathbb{R}^2$ such that for every edge $uv \in E$, the line segment connecting $f(u)$ and $f(v)$ does not intersect with any other edge's line segment except at its endpoints.
>
