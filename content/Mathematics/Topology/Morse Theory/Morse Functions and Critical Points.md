> [!definition] Critical Points
> Suppose $M$ is a [[Compactness of Topological Space#^da2511|compact]] smooth manifold


> [!definition] Morse Function
> A *Morse function* is a smooth map $f\colon M\to I$ where all its critical points are nondegenerate. When $M$ is a manifold with boundary, we also require that $f^{-1}(\partial I)= \partial M$.
