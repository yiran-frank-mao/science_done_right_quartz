> [!definition] Dihedral Group
> A *dihedral group* $D_{2n}$ is the group of symmetries of a regular $n$-sides polygon, which includes rotations and reflections with order $2n$: $$ D_{2n}=\langle r,s \mid r^n,s^{2}, (sr)^{2} \rangle. $$ ^0206e0


**Prop** $D_1$ and $D_2$ are the only abelian dihedral groups.
