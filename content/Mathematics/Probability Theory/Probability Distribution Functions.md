**Thrm**  <i><u>Transformation of PDF</u></i>
Call $𝑓(𝑥)$ the probability distribution function of the random variable $𝑋$. For a transformation of the form $𝑦=𝑦(𝑥)$, we have the following holds:$$
f(x)\dd x = f(y) \dd y
$$In other words, $$f(y)=f(x)\left|\frac{\dd x}{\dd y}\right|$$<b><u>e.g.</u></b>  Suppose $𝑋∼𝑢(0,1)$. For $𝑦=𝑒^𝑥$, we have $𝑥=\ln𝑦$ and then $\dd x / \dd y=1/𝑦$. Moreover $𝑓(𝑥)=1$, then the result is $𝑓(𝑦)=1/𝑦$.
