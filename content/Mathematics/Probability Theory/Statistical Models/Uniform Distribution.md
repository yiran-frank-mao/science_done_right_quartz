**Def**  <i><u>Uniform Distribution</u></i>
The probability density function of the continuous uniform distribution is: $$f(x)=\begin{cases}
\frac{1}{b-a} \quad &x\in[a,b]\\ 
0 \quad &\text{otherwise}
\end{cases}$$ And we write $X\sim \mathcal{U}(a,b)$ for random variable $X$.

**Prop**  Uniform distribution is in the symmetric family.

**Prop**  <i><u>Expectation and Variance of Uniform Distribution</u></i>
$$\mathbb{E}[X]=\frac{a+b}2\quad\mathrm{Var}(X)=\frac{(b-a)^2}{12}$$
