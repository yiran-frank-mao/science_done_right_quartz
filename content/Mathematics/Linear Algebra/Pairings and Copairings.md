> [!definition] Pairing
> A *pairing* of two vector spaces $V$ and $W$ is a linear map $\newcommand{\K}{\mathbb{K}}\beta\colon V \otimes W \to \K$.  It is sometimes convenient to adopt Dirac notation here and write $\beta(v\otimes w)=\braket{v|w}$.

> [!corollary]
> The obvious evaluation map $$V \otimes V^{*} \to \K, \qquad v \otimes \varphi \mapsto \varphi(v)$$ is nondegenerate if and only if $V$ is of finite dimension.
