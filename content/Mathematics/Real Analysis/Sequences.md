> [!definition] Sequence
> A sequence of real numbers is a [[Relations and Functions#^862dba|function]] $f\colon \N \to \R$, denoted $\{a_{n}\}_{n\in\N}$ with $a_{n}=f(n)$.

> [!definition] Increasing, Decreasing & Monotone
> A sequence $\{a_{n}\}$ over $\R$ is increasing if $a_{n}\leq a_{n+1}$, decreasing if $a_{n}\geq a_{n+1}$. $\{a_{n}\}$ is monotonic if it is either increasing or decreasing.

> [!definition] Alternating
> A sequence $\{a_{n}\}$ over $\R$ is alternating if $a_{n}\cdot a_{n+1}<0$ for all $n\in \N$.
