> [!definition] Representation of Lie Algebras
> Let $\newcommand{\g}{\mathfrak{g}}\g$ be a Lie algebra over a field $F$. A representation of $\g$ on a [[Vector Spaces#^f4b63e|vector space]] $V$ is a Lie algebra homomorphism $\newcommand{\gl}{\mathfrak{gl}}\rho\colon \g\to \gl(V)$ where $\gl(V)$ is the [Lie algebra of all linear endomorphisms on $V$](Lie%20Algebra#^d72504).
>
