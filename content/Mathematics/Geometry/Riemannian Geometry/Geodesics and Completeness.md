> [!definition] Riemannian Exponential Map
> Let $(M,g)$ be a semi-Riemannian manifold, and fix a point $p\in M$. The Riemannian exponential map $\exp_{p}\colon T_{p}M\to M$ sends a tangent vector $v$ to the point reached by following the geodesic starting at $p$ with initial velocity $v$, for a unit time.
