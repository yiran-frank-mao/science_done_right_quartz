> [!definition] Warped Product Manifold
> Suppose $(M,g_{M})$ and $(N,g_{N})$ are two semi-Riemannian manifolds, and $f\colon M\to \R$ is a smooth positive function. The warped product manifold $M\times_{f}N$ is the product manifold $M\times N$ with the metric defined by $$g=\pi_{M}^{*}(g_{M})+(f\circ\pi_{M})^{2}\pi_{N}^{*}(g_{N}).$$Explicitly, if $u,v\in T_{(p,q)}(M\times N)$, then $$g(u,v)=g_{M}(\d\pi_{M}(u),\d\pi_{M}(v))+f^{2}(p)g_{N}(\d\pi_{N}(u),\d\pi_{N}(v)).$$
