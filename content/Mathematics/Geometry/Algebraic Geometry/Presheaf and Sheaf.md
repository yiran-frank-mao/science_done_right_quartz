> [!definition] Presheaf
> A *presheaf* on a category $\mathsf{C}$ is a [[Functoriality#^653948|functor]] $\newcommand{\Set}{\mathsf{Set}}F\colon \mathsf{C}^{\text{op}}\to\Set$.
> In particular, if $\mathsf{C}$ is a topological space $X$ (i.e., the category of open sets of $X$), then a presheaf on $X$ is a functor $\mathsf{Open}(X)^{\text{op}}\to\Set$.
>
