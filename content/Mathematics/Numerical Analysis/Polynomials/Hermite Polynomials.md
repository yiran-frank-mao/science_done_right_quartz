
>[!definition] Hermite Polynomials
>Hermite polynomials is a polynomial that is defined by recurrence relation that: $$H_{n}(z)=2zH_{n-1}(z)-2(n-1)H_{n-2}(z),\quad H_{0}(z)=1$$Explicitly, we have $$\begin{aligned}&H_{0}(z)=1\\&H_{1}(z)=2z\\&H_{2}(z)=4z^{2}-2\\&H_{3}(z)=8z^{3}-12z\\&H_{4}(z)=16z^{4}-48z^{2}+12\\& \quad \vdots\end{aligned}$$ ^a81ecc
