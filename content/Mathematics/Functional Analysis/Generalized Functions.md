> [!definition] Distribution (Generalized Function)
> A *distribution* or *generalized function* is a continuous linear functional on $\mathscr{D}_{\Omega}$

<u><b>e.g.</b></u>
- **The Dirac Delta Distribution**: The familiar delta is not a classical function but is perfectly defined as a distribution. It “picks out” the value of a test function at zero: $$\langle \delta, \varphi \rangle := \varphi(0).$$More generally, the shifted delta at point $a$ works similarly: $\langle \delta_{a}, \varphi \rangle := \varphi(a)$.
