**Def**  <i><u>Riemann Zeta-Function</u></i>
The (real) Riemann zeta-function is defined as$$J(s)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^{s}}$$where $s\in\C$ with real part greater than $1$.

**Prop**  The radius of convergence of the series for the Riemann zeta-function is $s>1$.
