**Def**  <i><u>Prime-counting Function</u></i>
We define the prime counting function $\pi(x)$ to be the number of prime numbers that less than or equal to $x$.

**Thrm**  $\pi(x)\in\Theta(\frac{x}{\log x})$

**Def**  <i><u>$\mathrm{Li}$ Function</u></i>
We define a function, $$\mathrm{Li}(x)=\int_{2}^{x}\frac{\dd t}{\log t}$$
**Prop**  $\mathrm{Li}(x)>\pi(x)$ for $2\leq x\leq 10^{19}$, and $\mathrm{Li}(x)<\pi(x)$ for some $x\leq10^{137}$
