
## Shape-from-Shading

**Def**  <i><u>Shading</u></i>
Shading is the modulation of surface reflectance caused by the geometry of a scene, including surface orientation, light source characteristics, and the interaction of light with surfaces.

>[!remark]
>The following facts affect the shading of a surface:
>- The amount of light that falls on the surface;
>- The fraction of light that is reflected (albedo);
>- Geometry of light reflection: shape of the surface, orientation of the surface, and the direction of the light source, viewpoint.
>$\quad$
