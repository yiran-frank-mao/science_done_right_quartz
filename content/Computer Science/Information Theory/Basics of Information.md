>[!definition] Information
>Information is amount of unexpected (uncertain) data a message contains.

## General Communication System

>[!definition] General Communication System
>A general communication system consists of information source, transmitter, noise source, receiver and destination:
>![|400](https://i.imgur.com/RLa0rAy.png)

>[!definition] Channel
>A channel is some medium for transmitting messages. A noisy channel is a channel that potentially introduces errors in the sender's message.

>[!comment]
>The fundamental problem of communication is that of reproducing at one point either exactly or approximately on a message selected at another point.

## Uncertainty

>[!comment]
>We can avoid uncertainty when
>- dealing with noise (imperfections) in the channel.
>- compressing the messages.
>$\quad$

>[!comment]
>Information measures how much
>1. unexpected data a message contains.
>2. the receiver's uncertainty is reduced on seeing the message.
>$\quad$
